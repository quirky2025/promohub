## FAQ(2 条)
**Is laser engraving better than printing on metal pens?**
For longevity, yes — engraving cuts into the barrel so it can't wear off, and the tone-on-tone metal finish reads as premium. Choose pad printing when you need your exact brand colour instead.

**Can I get metal pens in a gift box?**
Yes — several of our metal pens come as boxed sets, or add a custom-branded gift box from our Packaging range for client-gift presentation.
