### 1. Logo placement is trending smaller & "retail-inspired" / logo 变小、走零售风
来源 Source:Counselor (ASI), Winter 2026, "Apparel Breakdown: Luxury Layering" — Kevin Crisafulli, Charles River Apparel

**Facts (EN)**: Classic left-chest spot remains common, but logos are trending **smaller, more tonal and more retail-inspired**. Designers experimenting with new placements — **bottom hems, biceps, cuffs, back shoulders** — to complement garment construction and seams.

中文解读:大 logo 时代过去了——高端企业服装的 logo 在变小、变低调、往零售成衣的感觉靠;位置也从左胸扩展到下摆/袖口/后肩。
可用于:AS Colour 计算器的印刷位置选项正好覆盖这些位置 = 文案卖点("choose from chest, hem, sleeve or back shoulder placements");apparel 深化页 "What's trending" 段。

### 2. Embroidery = gold standard for premium outerwear / 刺绣仍是高端外套的金标准
来源 Source:同上

**Facts (EN)**: Embroidery remains the "gold standard" for high-end jackets; heat-transfer emblems (PV emblems) gaining ground for depth and a retail look; **DTF (direct-to-film) transfers increasingly popular for small runs needing colour gradients or fine detail**.

中文解读:外套类首推刺绣(质感+耐久);小批量、渐变色 logo 用 DTF——正好呼应我们 MOQ 20 的小单印刷定位。
可用于:decoration-methods 页对比表;AS 外套/hoodie PDP 的方法推荐逻辑;FAQ "which print method for jackets?"。

### 3. Tone-on-tone decoration comeback / 同色调装饰回潮
来源 Source:同上 — Doug Jackson, Storm Creek

**Facts (EN)**: Tone-on-tone decoration making a comeback — subtle contrast key to legibility; increased demand for **matte heat-seals** and **tonal embroidery that matches the jacket's colour family** for a "classy, expensive" finish.

中文解读:同色系刺绣(navy 衣配深 navy 线)= "低调贵"。
可用于:文案钩子 *"tonal embroidery for a classy, expensive finish"*;给客户的 artwork 建议话术(proof 阶段可推荐)。

### 4. Winter apparel buying drivers / 冬装采购的三个驱动
来源 Source:同上 — Amy Leighton (Charles River), Doug Jackson & Chloe Ward (Storm Creek)

**Facts (EN)**: High-end winter jackets are about more than warmth — buyers want thoughtful features, sustainability and stretch. Sustainability now baked into top styles (recycled materials) without compromising performance. "Comfort as currency": soft fabrics = luxury indicator — "if you're going to spend $80 on a jacket, it should feel great." Feminine fits matter: women's jackets no longer "smaller men's jackets"; boxy fits and '90s silhouettes resonating. Employees pick from company merch stores what "feels current".

中文解读:①环保材质已是默认期待(接我们 Eco credentials);②手感=价值感;③女款要真女款版型(AS Colour 有 women's 线 = 卖点);④员工从企业 merch store 自选→"想穿才有效"——**直接支撑 YOUR MERCH STORE 的价值主张**。
可用于:apparel 深化页;YOUR MERCH STORE 说明页文案(员工自选、想穿的品牌服装);冬季 email/落地角度。
⚠ 注意:$80 jacket 是美国杂志语境,我们文案不搬价格,只搬"手感=价值感"的洞察。
