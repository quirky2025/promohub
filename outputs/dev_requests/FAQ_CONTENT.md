## Ordering(下单流程)

**1. How do I order custom promotional products?**
Browse our range and pick your product, then upload your logo or request a quote. We'll send you a free digital proof showing exactly how your logo will look. Once you approve the proof, we go into production and ship anywhere in Australia. → 内链:/faq 内锚点、Get a Quote、How to Order 内容页(第 6 类做好后补链)

**2. Can I see a proof before production starts?**
Yes — every order includes a free digital proof. Nothing goes to print until you've approved it, so you can request changes until it's exactly right. → 内链:artwork proof 说明(产品页 proof 区)

**3. How do I upload my logo, and what file formats do you accept?**
You can upload your logo on the product page or attach it to your quote request. Vector files (AI, EPS, PDF, SVG) give the sharpest print result. A high-resolution PNG also works — our team will check your file and let you know if we need anything better. → 内链:产品页上传入口

## Quantities(数量 / MOQ)

**4. What is the minimum order quantity?**
Custom-printed apparel starts at 20 units. For other products, the minimum is shown on each product page — many start lower. → 内链:类目页

**5. Can I mix sizes and colours in one order?**
You can mix sizes within the same colour to reach the minimum — for example, 20 tees in navy across S–XL. Different colours need to be ordered separately. → 内链:产品页尺码表

## Pricing & Payment(价格 / 付款)

**6. Do your prices include GST?**
Prices shown are ex-GST. GST (10%) is added at checkout or on your quote, itemised clearly so there are no surprises.

**7. Is there a setup fee for printing?**
Yes — a one-off setup fee applies per print position and method, and it's always shown as a separate line on your quote, never hidden in the unit price.

**8. How do I get a quote?**
Hit Get a Quote on any product (or the button in the menu), tell us your quantity and where you want your logo, and we'll come back to you within 3 business hours. → 内链:Get a Quote
(口径更新 2026-07-05:Lily 确认可做到 3 个工作小时,与页面侧边栏一致;原"当天回复"作废)

## Production & Turnaround(生产 / 交期)

**9. How long does production take?**
Custom-printed orders take 7–10 business days after you approve your artwork proof, plus shipping time. Need it sooner? Ask us when you enquire and we'll tell you what's possible.

**10. Can you match my exact brand colour (PMS)?**
Yes — send us your PMS colour code and we'll match it. Not sure of your code? Use our PMS chart to find it. → 内链:/resources/pms-chart

**11. What print methods do you offer, and which is best for my logo?**
We offer screen printing, pad printing, laser engraving, embroidery and more — the best method depends on your product and logo. See our decoration methods guide, or just send us your logo and we'll recommend one. → 内链:/services/decoration-methods

## Shipping & Returns(运费 / 售后)

**12. How much is shipping?**
$30 flat rate, Australia-wide — no matter how big your order is.

**13. What if my order arrives damaged or with a printing error?**
Contact us and we'll make it right in line with our Refund & Return policy. ⚠ 定稿措辞需与 /refund-return 页面现有条款一致 — 开发/Lily 核对后再上。→ 内链:/refund-return、/contact

## Products(产品)

**14. Can I buy products blank, without printing?**
Most of our range is available blank — check the product page. Our AS Colour apparel range is supplied custom-printed only. → 内链:类目页

**15. Do you offer eco-friendly promotional products?**
Yes — browse our Eco range. Many of our apparel products also carry credentials like amfori BSCI, PETA-Approved Vegan, UPF50+ and Australian Cotton, shown on each product page. → 内链:/eco(Eco 落地页)⚠ credentials 仅限带该标的产品,措辞用 "many" 不写 "all"
