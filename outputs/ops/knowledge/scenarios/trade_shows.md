### 1. Booth attention: what actually stops traffic / 展位抓注意力的 5 个实战招
来源 Source:Counselor (ASI), Winter/January 2026, "Ask Us Anything: How Do You Maximize Your Trade Show Experience?" — Corina Peacock, Founder/CEO, SpecWorks(资深 distributor 视角)

**Facts (EN)**:
- Clear signage on "what you do and why it matters"
- A focused display of best-sellers and newness — not everything
- **Live demos (especially decoration methods or quick product tests) stop traffic — "people can actually see the difference"**
- Simple takeaways; a QR code that isn't a scavenger hunt — speaks to pricing, timelines and use-cases without the hard sell
- Capture notes immediately after (photos and bullets), or the learning is lost

中文解读:清晰招牌讲清你是谁;聚焦陈列别摆满;**现场演示印刷工艺最截流**(呼应我们 free proof / 印刷对比的卖点);赠品要实用 + QR 直达价格交期;展后立刻记笔记。
可用于:落地页 "How to stand out at your next trade show" 段;giveaway 选品逻辑(实用>花哨)。
钩子 Hook:*"Live demos stop traffic — people want to see the difference."*

### 2. The 6-month ROI window / 展会回报的 6 个月时间窗
来源 Source:同上 — Jennifer Morales, Next Level Apparel(Top 40 supplier 视角)

**Facts (EN)**: "…evaluate the true return on investment, especially **within the six months following a trade show, when most business opportunities and follow-ups occur**." Industry pain point: no uniform, efficient lead-retrieval system with clear tracking.

中文解读:展会商机大多在**展后 6 个月内**发酵成交;行业普遍苦于 lead 追踪不统一。
可用于:落地页转化段核心钩子——展台三天,赠品替你跟进六个月(giveaway = 长效触点)。
⚠ 措辞红线:从业者陈述而非统计数据,写 *"industry veterans note…"*,不写 *"studies show"*。

### 3. Pre-show preparation / 展前准备清单
来源 Source:同上(两位受访者综合)

**Facts (EN)**: Set clear goals; train the team; booth reflects brand identity; well-displayed imagery + neatly presented product draw attendees; **follow up promptly with leads** and evaluate results after each show; go in with a game plan — a short list of must-see suppliers and target questions.

中文解读:目标清晰、团队培训、展位反映品牌、展后迅速 follow-up + 复盘。
可用于:blog《Trade show checklist for exhibitors》;落地页 FAQ "When should I order my trade show merch?"(倒推:展前数周下单,留足 proof + 生产时间)。

### 4a. Australian trade show calendar 2026 / 澳洲相关展会日历(时机弹药)
来源 Source:ASI Media, "The 2026 Trade Show Planning Guide" (asicentral.com, updated May 2026)

**Facts (EN)**:
- **AIME (Asia-Pacific Incentives and Meetings Event)** — Melbourne Convention Centre, Feb 9–11
- **Reed Gift Fairs Sydney** — ICC Sydney, Feb 14–17
- **Reed Gift Fairs Melbourne** — MCEC, **Aug 8–12**
- Industry association for Australia: **APPA (appa.com.au)**

中文解读:①我们的买家(参展商)全年有明确的展会节奏,2 月悉尼/墨尔本、8 月墨尔本是两个高峰;②**Reed Gift Fairs Melbourne 就在下个月(8 月)**——trade show 落地页和 email 角度现在推正是时候("Exhibiting at Reed Gift Fairs? Get your merch sorted — free proof, 7–10 day turnaround");③展前倒推下单窗口 = 我们内容的核心教育点;④APPA 是澳洲行业协会,以后做第三方提及/行业目录(GEO 第 3 条)时优先研究。
可用于:trade show 落地页的时效钩子;内容日历(每年 12–1 月、6–7 月为展前营销窗口);GEO 权威建设线索。

### 4b. How often buyers exhibit / 参展频率(内部背景知识)
来源 Source:同上

**Facts (EN)**: Veteran distributor: ~5 shows/year (one big national + mid-year + Q4 + regional). Leading supplier: 5 major + 15–20 regional shows/year.

中文解读:我们的目标买家一年参展多次 = **trade show 客户天然是复购客户**。
可用于:内部策略——trade show 落地页要接 reorder 逻辑;email 复购流里加"下一场展会"提醒角度。
